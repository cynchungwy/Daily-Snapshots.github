    4. HOW WE COLLECT YOUR DATA

    4.1 Data You Provide Directly

    We collect data that you actively provide to us, including when you

    register for an account, capture and upload photos or journal entries,

    make a purchase or subscription, contact us for support, or provide

    feedback or ratings.

    4.2 Data Collected Automatically

    When you use the Application, we and our service providers automatically

    collect certain technical data, including device identifiers, IP

    address, operating system version, app version, crash reports, and usage

    patterns. This data is collected using platform-standard analytics tools

    provided through our infrastructure partners (Base44, Supabase,

    MongoDB).

    4.3 Data From Third-Party Providers

    We receive limited data from our payment processor RevenueCat

    (subscription status, transaction IDs) and from Apple (App Store

    purchase verification). We do not receive raw payment card details —

    these are handled entirely by RevenueCat and Apple.
